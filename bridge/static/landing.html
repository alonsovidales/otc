Off The Cloud isn’t just another NAS or app — it’s a chance to reinvent the cloud and social networks as they should have been: private, family-friendly, and owned by the user.

We provide a small box you plug at home. Open the mobile app, pair via Bluetooth, set Wi-Fi and a username — and in moments your phone, laptops, and other devices start backing up photos, videos, and files. Share safely with family and friends, no ads, no tracking, no compression.

  • 🔒 Per-file encryption at rest; keys live only on your devices (Proton-style zero-access).
  • 🛡️ RAID-1 safety with automatic rebuilds and phone alerts.
  • 🧠 AI search on encrypted data: find “dogs on the beach” or “blue cars.”
  • 💶 1TB €250 · 2TB €300 · 4TB €450 — device + storage included.
  • 🌍 Direct access or our optional Bridge (€20/yr; first 2 years free for device buyers).
  • 🔓 100% open source. Your data never trains anyone’s models.

Why it’s different

Own your memories. Share in a safe, private social feed. Keep the quality (no forced compression) and the control.

Security

Zero-access encryption

Files are encrypted individually at rest. The box never stores your keys. If someone steals it, your data stays unreadable.

Setup

Plug, pair, done

Connect the box, pair via Bluetooth, set Wi-Fi and a unique username (your private handle). Auto-backup begins instantly.

Reliability

RAID-1 with auto-repair

Drive light turns green to red on failure; you get a phone alert; swap the disk and it rebuilds.

AI

Search your life

Semantic search over encrypted tensors. “Kids at school”, “mountain biking,” “sunset at the beach”—just type it.

Social

Family-first sharing

Instagram-like wall with likes & comments, minus toxicity, ads, and tracking. You choose what to show, and to whom.

Economy

Affordable by design

Modern, inexpensive hardware now runs strong crypto & AI. Storage is cheap — making true privacy finally accessible.

1

2

Open iOS/Android app → Bluetooth pairing → set Wi-Fi & username → done.

3
Auto-backup & share

Your phone & computers back up automatically. Share privately with family/friends.

4
Access anywhere
